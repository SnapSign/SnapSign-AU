## Summary Guidance
Summarize the key terms of the employment offer.

## Risk Analysis
- Highlight any "at-will" employment clauses.
- Check for non-compete or non-solicitation clauses.
- Verify if the salary and benefits match standard expectations for the role.

## Extraction Targets
- Employer Name
- Job Title
- Salary/Compensation
- Start Date
- Reporting To
