## Summary Guidance
Explain the nature of the notice or offence. State clearly if a fine is due or an action is required.

## Risk Analysis
- Check for deadlines to pay or appeal.
- Highlight consequences of non-payment (e.g., license suspension).

## Extraction Targets
- Offence Description
- Fine Amount
- Due Date
- Reference Number
