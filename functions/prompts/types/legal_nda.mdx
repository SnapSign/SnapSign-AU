## Summary Guidance
Explain what information is being protected and for how long.

## Risk Analysis
- Check if the definition of "Confidential Information" is too broad.
- Verify the duration of the confidentiality obligation.
- Ensure there are standard exclusions (e.g., public information).

## Extraction Targets
- Disclosing Party
- Receiving Party
- Purpose
- Duration
