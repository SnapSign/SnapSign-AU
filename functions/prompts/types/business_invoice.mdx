## Summary Guidance
Identify the goods/services provided and the amount due.

## Risk Analysis
- Verify if the tax calculation is correct (e.g., GST/VAT).
- Check payment terms and late fees.

## Extraction Targets
- Vendor Name
- Invoice Date
- Invoice Number
- Total Amount
- Due Date
