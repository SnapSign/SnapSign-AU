## The Problem
Business documents are often:
- complex
- poorly written
- full of legal or technical language

If English is not your first language, this gets even harder.

---

## How DecoDocs Helps
- Explains documents in simple language
- Translates documents or sections when needed
- Shows explanations side-by-side with the original text

You don't need to "interpret" anything yourself.

---

## What You Get
- Plain-language explanations
- Clear understanding of responsibilities
- Fewer misunderstandings with clients or partners
